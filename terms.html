Editron Terms of Service

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Editron, the video editing platform that connects clients with professional video editors. By using our platform, you agree to these Terms of Service and our Privacy Policy.

Important: These terms include our commission structure, payment policies, and cancellation procedures. Please read them carefully.

2. Platform Commission Structure

Editron charges a platform commission to maintain our services and ensure quality experiences for both clients and editors.

2.1 Standard Commission

For all completed projects, Editron charges a 10% commission on the total project value.

2.2 Loyalty Program

Editors who complete 5+ successful deals with positive ratings qualify for our Silver Level program, which reduces the commission to 5%.

2.3 Platform Fee

An additional 5% platform fee is applied to all payments to cover payment processing and platform maintenance costs.

3. Payment & Advance Policy

3.1 Advance Payment Requirement

To initiate any project, clients must pay an advance of 20% of the total project value. This advance serves as:

  • Commitment to the project
  • Platform fee coverage
  • Initial payment to the editor

3.2 Payment Release

Remaining payments are released to editors upon project completion and client approval. Editron holds funds in escrow to ensure both parties fulfill their obligations.

4. Cancellation & Refund Policy

4.1 Client-Initiated Cancellation

If a client cancels a project after paying the advance but before work has started:

  • Platform fee (5%) is non-refundable
  • Cancellation fee (2%) is applied
  • Remaining 13% is refunded to the client

4.2 Editor-Initiated Cancellation

If an editor cannot complete a project after accepting it:

  • Full advance amount is refunded to the client
  • Editor's reputation score is affected
  • Repeated cancellations may lead to account suspension

4.3 Work-in-Progress Cancellation

If work has already started, cancellation terms are negotiated based on the completed work percentage.

5. Project Workflow

5.1 Standard Workflow

  1. Client posts project with requirements and budget
  2. Editors submit proposals
  3. Client selects editor and pays 20% advance
  4. Project tracker is automatically created
  5. Editor delivers work according to milestones
  6. Client reviews and provides feedback
  7. Final delivery and payment release

5.2 Milestone System

All projects include milestone tracking to ensure transparency and timely delivery. Both parties can monitor progress through the project timeline.

6. Communication & Privacy

6.1 Secure Messaging

Editron provides a secure messaging system that:

  • Filters sensitive contact information until advance payment is made
  • Maintains communication records for dispute resolution
  • Protects both parties from spam and unsolicited contact

6.2 Contact Information Protection

Personal contact information (phone numbers, email addresses, social media profiles) is automatically filtered from messages until the advance payment is confirmed.

7. Editor Reputation System

7.1 Reputation Points

Editors earn reputation points based on:

  • Completed projects: +10 points
  • Cancelled projects: -5 points
  • Late deliveries: -5 points

7.2 Reputation Levels

  • Bronze (0-50 points): Standard 10% commission
  • Silver (51-100 points): Reduced 5% commission
  • Gold (101+ points): Featured placement, 5% commission

8. Dispute Resolution

In case of disputes between clients and editors:

  1. Parties should first attempt to resolve issues through communication
  2. If unresolved, either party can escalate to Editron support
  3. Editron will review project details, communication, and deliverables
  4. Final decisions are made based on platform policies and fairness

9. Account Termination

Editron reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts for:

  • Violation of these terms
  • Fraudulent activities
  • Multiple unresolved disputes
  • Attempts to bypass platform payments
